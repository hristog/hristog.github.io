This entry is probably a bit on the short side for a table of contents, but Chert makes it easy to add one like the one above. Simply include [TOC] in your entry, wherever you'd like the Contents to appear. Also note that regardless of whether a table of contents is generated, headings always link to themselves, making anchor retrieval and navigation even more convenient.
